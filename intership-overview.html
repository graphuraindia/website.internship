Virtual Internship Program Overview - Graphura India Private Limited

In today’s digital-first world, internships are no longer just résumé fillers—they’re stepping stones toward building future-ready skills. Graphura India Private Limited, a creative agency at the intersection of design, technology, and strategy, has launched a 3-month Virtual Internship Program to give students and fresh graduates hands-on exposure to real projects—all remotely.

Duration: 3 Months
Mode: 100% Remote (Work from Home)
Eligibility: Students, graduates, and young professionals eager to learn.
Certificate: Awarded after successful completion.

📅 Program Details
💼 Internship Domains Offered
1. Human Resources (HR) Internship

Interns in HR gain experience in recruitment, employee relations, and organizational development. You’ll assist with resume screening, scheduling interviews, and supporting onboarding processes. This track is ideal for those who want to understand workplace culture and people management in a corporate setup.

2. Graphic Design Internship

A creative pathway where interns use tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Canva to design logos, social media creatives, infographics, and brand collaterals. Perfect for students passionate about visual communication and brand storytelling.

3. Data Analytics & Data Science Internship

This domain offers exposure to data cleaning, visualization, and entry-level machine learning models. Interns work with Excel, Python, SQL, and Tableau/Power BI to uncover insights. It’s designed for those who love numbers, problem-solving, and decision-making through data.

4. Social Media Management Internship

Here, interns manage brand presence across platforms like Instagram, LinkedIn, and Twitter. You’ll create content calendars, track performance metrics, and learn how to grow digital communities through strategy and creativity.

5. Content Writing Internship

Develop skills in SEO writing, blog development, ad copy, and storytelling. Interns learn how to create content that not only informs but also engages and converts. A great fit for those with a knack for words and creativity.

6. UI/UX Design Internship

This domain focuses on creating user-friendly interfaces and smooth digital experiences. Interns explore wireframing, prototyping, user research, and usability testing using tools like Figma and Adobe XD. Ideal for those passionate about design and technology.

7. Digital Marketing Internship

Interns gain hands-on knowledge of SEO, SEM, email marketing, paid campaigns, and analytics tools like Google Ads and Google Analytics. The focus is on creating measurable growth strategies that brands can rely on.

8. Web Development Internship

An opportunity to work on both front-end and back-end development. Interns code using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Node.js, while also exploring CMS platforms. You’ll learn to create responsive, functional, and user-centric websites.

9. Video Editing Internship

Interns edit reels, advertisements, and explainer videos using tools like Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, or Final Cut Pro. This role develops storytelling skills through motion graphics, transitions, and creative cuts.

10. Sales and Marketing Internship

A track focused on lead generation, pitching, and building client relationships. Interns learn to conduct market research, identify potential customers, and craft effective sales strategies. Perfect for aspiring entrepreneurs and business leaders.

11. Journalism & Mass Communication Internship

Interns here sharpen skills in news reporting, article writing, PR content creation, and storytelling. The focus is on delivering information clearly, engagingly, and ethically across different media formats.

12. Finance Internship

In this domain, interns gain exposure to budgeting, financial analysis, and accounting fundamentals. You’ll work with Excel, Tally, or QuickBooks to prepare financial reports and forecasts, preparing you for roles in corporate finance or banking.

13. Data Scraping Internship

A technical role where interns use Python (BeautifulSoup, Scrapy, Selenium) to extract and structure data from websites and APIs. This skill is increasingly in demand for research, marketing intelligence, and automation projects.

🚀 Why Join Graphura’s Internship Program?

Work Flexibly: 100% online, accessible from anywhere.
Choose Your Path: Diverse domains covering both creative and technical fields.
Hands-On Experience: Work on live projects, not just theory.
Industry-Relevant Skills: Gain insights that align with market needs.
Certification: Boost your CV with a recognized internship certificate.
